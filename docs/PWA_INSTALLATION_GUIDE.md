# Install BOPA on a phone or computer

BOPA can be installed from the website. It does not require an app-store download.

## Windows or macOS

Use Chrome or Microsoft Edge.

1. Open `boldverseproperty.com`.
2. Sign in to the correct BOPA workspace.
3. Choose **Install BOPA** when it appears.
4. Approve the browser installation.
5. Open BOPA from the Start menu, desktop, Applications folder, or Dock.

The installed version opens in its own app window.

## Android

Use Chrome.

1. Open `boldverseproperty.com`.
2. Sign in.
3. Tap **Install BOPA** when it appears.
4. Approve the installation.
5. Open BOPA from the Home Screen or app list.

## iPhone

Use Safari.

1. Open `boldverseproperty.com` in Safari.
2. Sign in.
3. Tap the Share button.
4. Choose **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Keep **Open as Web App** enabled when the option is shown.
6. Tap **Add**.

Open BOPA from the Home Screen.

## iPad

Use Safari.

1. Open `boldverseproperty.com`.
2. Tap Share.
3. Choose **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Enable **Open as Web App** when shown.
5. Tap **Add**.

## Before relying on offline access

Open the Manager or Developer workspace once while connected to the internet. BOPA will keep a protected, read-only operational copy on that device.

Only approved maintenance and descriptive/contact changes can be saved offline. Payments, bank details, KYC, agreements, receipts, reports, private documents, tenant activation, plot allocation, pricing, and sale confirmation require an internet connection.

## When the app says the copy may be out of date

Reconnect and open BOPA. The app refreshes automatically.

## When changes are waiting

Keep BOPA open after reconnecting. It will save queued changes automatically.

## When attention is needed

Open **Review** and choose whether to keep the online version or use the changes saved on the device.

## Removing BOPA from a device

Open **Offline storage** and clear the offline copy after all waiting changes are saved. Then uninstall the app through the device's normal app removal controls.
